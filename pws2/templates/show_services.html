Services on the Go

Work schedules and after-school activities can make it hard to find time for regular weekly appointments. Yet consistent therapy is important for your child’s success. That’s why Prairie Wind offers offsite services at your child’s school or daycare. Call today to inquire.

Note that we are unable to bill insurance for offsite services, but you will receive our discounted rate (50% off) for paying out-of- pocket. All offsite services are contingent on the approval of the school or daycare center. There is a $20 surcharge for offsite services.

Flexible Scheduling

Family schedules often change from month to month. We work with you to accommodate your busy lifestyle. At Prairie Wind you are not locked in to a rigid schedule and we will work with your schedule to arrange appointments or provide therapy in your child’s school or daycare. Call today and let’s talk about how we can work with you.
